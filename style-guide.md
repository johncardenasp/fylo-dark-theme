## Typography

### Body Copy

- Font size: 14px

### Headings, Call-to-actions, Header Navigation

- Family: [Raleway](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Raleway)
- Weights: 400, 700

### Body

- Family: [Open Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Open+Sans)
- Weights: 400, 700
